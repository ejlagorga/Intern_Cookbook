Fajitas Chicken

Ingredients

  1. 1 lbs Chicken
  2. 1 Pepper
  3. 1 Onion
  4. 4 Tortillas
  5. 1/2 cup Cheese
  6. 3 tbsp Oil
  7. 1 tsp Salt
  8. 1 tsp Chilepowder
  9. 1/2 tsp Cumin
  10. 1/2 tsp Onionpowder
  11. 1/2 tsp Garlicpowder
  12. 1 tbsp Cornstarch
  13. 2 tbsp Limejuice

Directions

  1. Pour 1/4 cup of water into resealable plastic bag. Add Chile powder, Salt, Cumin, Onion powder, Garlic powder and Onion. Seal and knead gently. Refigerate for 15 min.
  2. Heat oil in a large non stick skillet over high heat. Empty bag into skillet and cook until chicken is cooked through. Remove heat and stir in lime juice.
  3. Transfer chicken and veggies to tortillas and add cheese.
