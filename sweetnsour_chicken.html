Sweet and Sour Chicken

Ingredients

  1. 1 lbs Chicken
  2. 1 Pepper
  3. 1 cup Rice
  4. 1 tbsp Oil
  5. 3 tbsp Butter
  6. 1 tbs Salt
  7. 3 tbsp Honey
  8. 1 tbsp Garlic
  9. 1 tbsp Vinegar

Directions

  1. Cube chicken into bit-sized pieces. Start cooking rice.
  2. Heat oil and butter in a large non stick skillet over high heat.
  3. When the butter is melted, add the chicken. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, add garlic, vinegar, and honey.
  4. When chicken is done, remove chicken and add pepper. Bring to a simmer until sauce has carmelized.
